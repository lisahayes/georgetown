What the 78626 ZIP covers

The 78626 postal area takes in east and central Georgetown, which is to say the oldest part of the city. It wraps around the historic limestone courthouse square and Old Town, runs through Downtown and its businesses, and reaches the established neighborhoods on the east side, including San Gabriel Estates, Wood Ranch, and the homes near Garey Park. If your address is in 78626, you are most likely in an older or established part of Georgetown, and that shapes the plumbing we are likely to find.

We serve the whole ZIP, residential and commercial, and treat each property according to its age rather than a single template.

The oldest housing stock in the city

Because 78626 holds Georgetown's earliest neighborhoods, it carries the city's oldest plumbing materials: galvanized steel supply that rusts and chokes pressure, and cast-iron or clay sewer and drain lines that crack and scale with age. Mid-century and later homes in the established east-side neighborhoods add aging copper to the mix. So a leak in 78626 might be a rusted galvanized line in a historic home, a corroded cast-iron drain, or a copper pinhole in a mid-century house.

Reading which one a property has is the starting point, and the variety here is exactly why we do not assume.

Homes and businesses, both served

The 78626 ZIP is unusual in mixing a busy commercial district with historic and established homes. Around the square and Downtown, we handle commercial plumbing for shops, offices, and restaurants, keeping the work area small and scheduling around operating hours where the leak allows. In the surrounding neighborhoods, we handle residential leaks in homes that range from century-old to mid-century, with the care each calls for.

Both share Georgetown's very-hard water and the older lines that come with an established area, so corrosion, scale, and root intrusion are common threads across the ZIP.

Mature trees and aging laterals

The established lots across 78626 are shaded by mature trees, and those roots chase moisture into any crack in an aging sewer lateral, making root intrusion a frequent cause of slow drains and backups here. A camera inspection is the honest way to read a lateral that has been in the ground for decades, showing its real condition rather than leaving us to guess.

Between old supply pipe, aging drains, and root-prone sewers, the 78626 area keeps a varied list of leak sources, and we bring the right tool for each.

How we serve 78626

Across this ZIP we locate leaks before opening anything, acoustic and thermal for supply and slab leaks, a camera for the old sewer and drain lines, and we match the repair to the material and its condition. For historic homes we work to disturb as little as possible, and for businesses we keep disruption down and respond around the clock when a leak threatens operations.

If a property anywhere in 78626 shows rusty water, slow drains, a sewer smell, or a leak you cannot place, we cover the whole ZIP and will find it. Old homes and businesses alike get the same precise, locate-first approach, so the search ends at one small opening rather than a torn-up wall or floor.
